# Project History

# The Beginning of NebulaStack

The story of NebulaStack began long before the current portfolio concept.

During the rapid growth of Telegram-based Tap-to-Earn (TTE) projects, I became interested in blockchain technologies and started studying how cryptocurrencies and decentralized ecosystems work.

As an experiment, I created a concept for a digital token under the name **NebulaStack** and designed its first logo. The project was planned for the Blum platform and became my first attempt to build something of my own.

Although the experiment was valuable, I eventually realized that blockchain was not the direction I truly wanted to pursue. Instead of abandoning the idea completely, I paused the project.

Some time later, NebulaStack returned with a completely different purpose — not as a cryptocurrency, but as a personal IT ecosystem focused on software development, engineering, research, and documenting my journey as a developer.

Today, this concept defines NebulaStack.

# The Name "NebulaStack"

Choosing the name was an important step.

I wanted it to reflect both limitless opportunities and a strong technological foundation.

**Nebula** symbolizes vastness, curiosity, and continuous exploration. It represents the idea that there are always new directions to discover within technology.

**Stack** refers to the technological side of the project. It reflects programming, engineering, software stacks, and the systematic accumulation of knowledge.

Together, the name **NebulaStack** represents continuous growth, exploration, and the construction of an ever-expanding personal ecosystem.

# The Evolution of the Logo

The NebulaStack logo has evolved alongside the project itself.

Its purpose has always remained the same: to visually represent the philosophy behind NebulaStack.

## Generation I — Cryptocurrency Concept

The first logo was created for the original blockchain idea.

It featured a stylized digital black hole together with the NebulaStack name, emphasizing technology and decentralization.

## Generation II — Portfolio Prototype

When NebulaStack transformed into a development portfolio, the logo was redesigned.

It consisted of two intertwined Python snakes surrounding a black hole.

The design adopted a clean minimalist style on a white background and symbolized programming as the project's foundation.

## Generation III — Current Identity

The current logo became the visual identity of the entire ecosystem.

It combines a black hole with an accretion disk that naturally transforms into the body of a Python snake.

The logo is executed in a minimalist monochrome style on a black background and represents the central philosophy of NebulaStack:

**Knowledge attracts knowledge.**

Just as a black hole grows by attracting surrounding matter, NebulaStack continuously expands by absorbing new technologies, projects, and experience.
